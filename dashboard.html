What "API wall time" means here. Each session transcript is replayed and three durations are computed: Active time — the sum of gaps between successive events with idle gaps (> ) removed; a uniform, idle-free estimate available for every session (calibrated to ≈1.08× of Claude's own turn timing). Turn time — the sum of Claude Code's recorded turn_duration values (real per-turn wall clock, including in-turn waits); shown for the sessions that recorded it, falling back to active time otherwise. Calendar span — first to last event, including all idle. Heatmap & calendar always use idle-free active time. Sessions marked est had no turn timing recorded.
